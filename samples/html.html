HTML

  • Element: these are the 'parts' of a page e.g. body or h1 
            
          <html>
            <head>
              <title>My Web Page</title>
            </head>
            <body>
              <h1>Welcome to my web page!</h1>
              <p>This is a paragraph of text.</p>
            </body>
          </html>
  • Attribute: these are values within specific Elements
